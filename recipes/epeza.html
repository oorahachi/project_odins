Epe eza

The Ebira beans soup remains some of the best delicacy in Okene. Here are some ingredients needed for it

  • beans
  • dried fish
  • palmoil
  • pepper (ata rodo)
  • whatever the ancestors reveal!

Steps

  1. ground beans
  2. put beans on heat, include pepper and onoins
  3. include dried fish
  4. include palmoil
  5. let food simmer
  6. enjoy!
