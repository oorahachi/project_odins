Ebira beans and plantain

The Ebira beans and plantain remains some of the best delicacy in Okene. Here are some ingredients needed for it

  • beans
  • plantain
  • dried fish
  • palmoil
  • pepper (ata rodo)
  • whatever the ancestors reveal!

Steps

  1. put beans on heat, include pepper and onoins
  2. let beans soften, include plantain
  3. include dried fish
  4. include palmoil
  5. let food simmer
  6. enjoy!
